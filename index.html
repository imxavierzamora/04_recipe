Trader X's Mai Tai

a twist on Trader Vic's 1944 Oakland classic


Recipe

  • 1 oz Amber Martinique Rum
  • 1 oz Dark Jamaican Rum
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz Orgeat Syrup
  • 11/2 oz Cointreau
  • garnish with mint (or lime)

  1. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker, except the garnish.
  2. Shake and strain into a rocks glass filled with crushed ice.
  3. Garnish with fresh mint and a lime if you fancy that.
  4. You can also float some dark rum on top of the cocktail.
